Story
Once upon a time… our beloved Cuphead was walking through Botanic Fields. On his way back to Elder Kettle to receive his finger gun powers, Cuphead encounters a weird blockage.
He is forced to take an alternate… rough looking type of road. As he continues to walk he finds himself trapped in an endless large stone road area. It's a trap!
Out of the shadows, Dice King reveals himself! “Well lookee here,” he says. “You’re still alive and making me Lose a bet.” “And for that, you can’t go home yet! We’re gonna play a little Game first. And you’re the main star. Lucky for you, you can get out of my endless trap by finding the real stars among these illusions. If you don't get out in a few seconds... you'll be trapped FOREVER!!”
